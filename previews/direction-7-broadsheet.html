About This Site
By Derek Brimley · April 2026

I'm a software engineer based in Salt Lake City, Utah. This is where I keep a record of the projects I've built, the books I've read, the films I've watched, and the concerts I've attended.

The logbook is a personal practice — a habit of noticing what moves through my life and leaving a trace of it behind. The projects section documents what I've made and shipped.

Current Work

Currently focused on product engineering — the kind of work that sits at the intersection of technical execution and understanding what people actually need from software.

I'm interested in tools that feel genuinely useful rather than impressive: software that recedes into the background and lets the work happen.
